Description

Makes an invisible or transparent material act like an opaque material.

Usage

In the global section of your material shader type... 

forceOpaque

Parameters

None.

Notes

Used in materials like caulk (to make them seal off the void) or in sky materials. (so transparent textures, like windows, will always draw on top of the sky material)
